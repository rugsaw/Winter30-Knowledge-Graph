Corporate Overview:
XYZ Global Holdings Limited (“XYZ Global”, “the Group”, or “the Company”) is a multinational conglomerate incorporated in India, with significant operations across energy, infrastructure, consumer retail, digital services, financial services, and advanced materials. The Group operates in over 38 countries and employs approximately 412,000 people globally.
For the financial year ended March 31, 2025 (FY 2024–25), XYZ Global reported consolidated revenues of INR 11.46 trillion, compared to INR 10.12 trillion in FY 2023–24, representing a year-on-year increase of 13.2%. Consolidated EBITDA increased to INR 1.94 trillion, while profit after tax attributable to equity shareholders stood at INR 912 billion.

Ownership Structure and Group Entities:
XYZ Global Holdings Limited functions as the ultimate parent entity and directly or indirectly controls over 120 subsidiaries, joint ventures, and associates.
Key operating entities include:
	• XYZ Energy & Chemicals Limited (100% subsidiary)
	• XYZ Retail & Consumer Services Limited (92.4% subsidiary)
	• XYZ Digital Networks Limited (83.1% subsidiary)
	• XYZ Infrastructure Projects Limited (75% subsidiary)
	• XYZ Green Future Energy Limited (67% subsidiary)
	• XYZ Capital & Financial Services Limited (58% subsidiary)
The Group also holds minority strategic stakes in:
	• NovaPolymers BV (Netherlands) – 26% equity interest
	• IndoPacific LNG Terminal LLP – 40% partnership interest

Energy, Chemicals & Materials Segment:
The Energy, Chemicals & Materials segment contributed INR 6.02 trillion to consolidated revenues, accounting for 52.5% of the Group’s total revenue.
Refining & Fuels
XYZ Energy & Chemicals Limited operates three refining complexes located in Jamnagar, Paradip, and Fujairah, with a combined crude processing capacity of 2.3 million barrels per day.
During FY 2024–25:
	• Average crude throughput was 2.18 million barrels per day
	• Average capacity utilization stood at 109%
	• Gross refining margin averaged USD 10.4 per barrel, compared to USD 8.9 per barrel in FY 2023–24
Benchmark Brent crude prices averaged USD 84 per barrel during the year, compared to USD 91 per barrel in the previous fiscal year.

Petrochemicals & Polymers:
Global ethylene demand increased by 2.6% in calendar year 2024, reaching approximately 187 million metric tonnes. Global polymer demand grew by 3.4% to 261 million metric tonnes.
Domestic polymer demand in India grew by 7.9% in FY 2024–25, driven by packaging, automotive, and infrastructure sectors. Polypropylene demand grew faster than polyethylene, registering growth of 9.1% compared to 6.3%.
XYZ Energy & Chemicals Limited commissioned a new polypropylene plant with a capacity of 0.9 million tonnes per annum in Q3 FY 2024–25, at a capital cost of INR 146 billion.

Retail, Consumer & Lifestyle Segment:
XYZ Retail & Consumer Services Limited operates a nationwide omni-channel retail network comprising:
	• 21,700 physical stores
	• 1.32 billion square feet of retail space
	• Presence across grocery, apparel, electronics, pharmacy, home, and luxury formats
Retail revenues for FY 2024–25 stood at INR 3.84 trillion, reflecting a growth of 18.9% over the previous year.
The Group’s digital commerce platform, ShopSphere, recorded:
	• Gross merchandise value (GMV) of INR 612 billion
	• Order volume growth of 31%
	• Average order value of INR 2,480
During the year, XYZ Retail acquired a 51% controlling stake in Heritage Foods & Lifestyle Private Limited for a consideration of INR 18.6 billion.

Digital Networks & Telecommunications:
XYZ Digital Networks Limited operates one of the largest integrated digital connectivity platforms in Asia.
As of March 31, 2025:
	• Subscriber base: 489 million users
	• Average revenue per user (ARPU): INR 201 per month
	• Monthly data consumption per user: 31.4 GB
Telecom segment revenues increased to INR 1.46 trillion, while EBITDA margins improved from 44.2% to 47.1%.
Capital expenditure in this segment amounted to INR 418 billion, primarily toward 5G rollout, fiber expansion, and data center capacity augmentation.

Infrastructure & Logistics:
XYZ Infrastructure Projects Limited executes large-scale infrastructure projects including ports, airports, roads, and logistics parks.
Key metrics:
	• 11 operational ports handling 312 million metric tonnes of cargo annually
	• 6 international airports under operation and management
	• Logistics warehousing capacity of 92 million square feet
Infrastructure segment revenues stood at INR 612 billion, growing at 14.7% year-on-year.

Renewable Energy & Sustainability:
XYZ Green Future Energy Limited focuses on solar, wind, battery storage, and green hydrogen projects.
As of FY 2024–25:
	• Installed renewable capacity: 8.9 GW
	• Projects under development: 12.6 GW
	• Green hydrogen pilot capacity: 45 kilotonnes per annum
The Group committed INR 742 billion toward renewable and low-carbon initiatives over the next five years.

Financial Services:
XYZ Capital & Financial Services Limited operates across lending, insurance distribution, asset management, and payments.
Key financial metrics:
	• Assets under management: INR 4.62 trillion
	• Loan book size: INR 2.18 trillion
	• Gross NPA ratio: 2.1%
	• Capital adequacy ratio: 18.4%
The entity is regulated by the Reserve Bank of India and other applicable financial authorities.

Geographic Revenue Mix:
	• India: 68%
	• Asia (ex-India): 14%
	• Europe: 9%
	• North America: 7%
	• Middle East & Africa: 2%
European revenues declined by 4.1% due to inflationary pressures, while North American revenues grew by 6.8%.

Risk Factors and Uncertainties:
The Group faces several material risks:
	• Volatility in crude oil, natural gas, and polymer prices
	• Interest rate fluctuations affecting borrowing costs
	• Regulatory changes in telecom spectrum pricing
	• Climate transition risks and carbon pricing mechanisms
	• Cybersecurity threats across digital platforms
	• Litigation risks related to land acquisition for infrastructure projects

Capital Structure & Financing:
As of March 31, 2025:
	• Gross debt: INR 4.28 trillion
	• Cash and equivalents: INR 1.54 trillion
	• Net debt-to-EBITDA ratio: 1.4x
During FY 2024–25:
	• Bonds issued: INR 386 billion
	• Loans repaid: INR 274 billion
	• Equity infused into subsidiaries: INR 192 billion

Forward-Looking Statements:
The Group expects:
	• Domestic energy demand growth of 5–6% annually
	• Retail consumption growth to outpace GDP by 300 basis points
	• Renewable capacity to exceed 20 GW by FY 2027–28
	• Consolidated capex of INR 1.32 trillion in FY 2025–26
However, these projections are subject to macroeconomic conditions, geopolitical developments, and regulatory outcomes.
